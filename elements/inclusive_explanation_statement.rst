Inclusive Explanation Statement
^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^^

In this course, each voice in the classroom has something of value to contribute.
Please take care to respect the different experiences, beliefs, and values
expressed by students and staff involved in this course. We support Simpson's
commitment to diversity, and welcome individuals of all ages, backgrounds,
citizenships, disabilities, sex, education, ethnicities, family statuses,
genders, gender identities, geographical locations, languages, military
experience, political views, races, religions, sexual orientations,
socioeconomic statuses, and work experiences.
